Variety of Card Sizes

Our cards come in a number of industry standard sizes that you can pick from. Some of the most popular ones include: Pano: 4" x 9", A-6: 4.5" x 6.25" and A-1: 3.5" x 4.875". Contact us for more size options.

Competitive Pricing

We pride ourselves on being a cost-effective way to decorate your room in a way that represents you. When you pick one of our basic card sizes and an image from our galaxy, our cards run anywhere from $2.50-$5.25 a card. Custom sizing and images may increase the price some depending on the complexity of the image or the sizing. There are also discounts available for bundle ordering, if you order 10 or more cards at a time you get a $5 discount. Orders over 25 items will get a 12$ discount.

Simple Ordering

When you order from us, all you have to do is provide two things: the code that corresponds with the image you like and the size that you'd like your card. If you're provding an image to us, there's just the extra step of uploading that image so that we have it. We endeavor to keep our ordering process as efficient and simple as possible which is made possible through using image codes and card codes.

Custom Image Printing

For a slight up-charge, you can provide images to us to be printed on a card size of your choosing. These images should have a similar aspect ratio to that of the card size you want to use. For example: an image you want printed on a Pano sized card should have an aspect ratio of 4:9.
